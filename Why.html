Why should you care at all?

Some people have a hard time controlling their spending habits. You might be thinking, but what if I don’t spend money and I’m super frugal? Well that also affects you because it can affect the way publishers monetize in games. Take the video game Shadows of War for example. The game started with loot boxes that can give in game Orcs of various high level. This also affected the end game content because the enemies were very high leveled compared to what you can do in game. The boxes were eventually pulled and all the content was rescaled better. But it left a bitter taste in players’ minds for weeks after the game’s launch. And there are probably people who already spent some money on the boxes before they were pulled.
